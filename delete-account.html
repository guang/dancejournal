Delete your Dance Notes account

Last updated: May 10, 2026

Dance Notes is published by Dance Journal. If you'd like to delete your account and the data associated with it, you can do so directly from inside the app.

How to delete your account

  1. Open Dance Notes on your phone.
  2. Tap your profile icon (top-right) to open the user menu.
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. You may be asked to sign in again — this is required by our identity provider for security-sensitive actions.

The deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

  • Your account credentials (email, name, profile photo) from our authentication system
  • Your video clips, thumbnails, and any uploaded media you own
  • Your notes, progress, favorites, and collections
  • Your push notification tokens
  • Your dance preferences and settings

What we retain (and for how long)

  • Encrypted database backups are retained for up to 30 days as part of our standard disaster-recovery process, after which they are permanently destroyed.
  • Anonymous, aggregated usage data (no personal information) may be retained indefinitely for product improvement.
  • Legal compliance records may be retained where required by law.

Questions?

If you have questions about deletion or your data, contact us at guang [at] dancejournal [dot] app.
